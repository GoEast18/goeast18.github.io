Stuttgart - Vladivostok 2018

Day 62, Rubtsovsk (Russia) - Barnaul (Russia)

Date Do 06 September 2018 By Helmut Dittrich, Translation by Julie Herberger-Dittrich Category english Tags 09. Week / Rubtsovsk / Russia / West-Siberia / Barnaul / tire search

It is 11 degrees celcius, blue sky, sunshine, super road, beautiful color contrasts of agricultural land. These are large tracts of land laid out over vast areas and are often bordered by the road and forest edges.

The truck stop has western standards and is very clean and modern. I order Soljanka and Laghman to taste these dishes now in Russia. Delicious. The staff can speak a few words of German here in Western Siberia. A guest also says goodbye. Probably because around Barnaul around 70,000 Russian Germans are living.

Thanks to the excellent road and the most beautiful weather with pleasant temperatures, we can quickly get to Barnaul to look for a suitable tire for Charlie. Finally, we meet Andrew, a motorcycle fanatic who even gives Charlie a used tire.

We go to a shopping center and get us SIM cards for our mobile phones. Converted 7.50 EUR for one month with unlimited data volume and 500 free minutes to make calls. Hello Germany, am I ripped off at home? Very obviously.

Unfortunately, it was too late to continue and so we are looking for a hotel, which we also find quite central. From Andre and Martin we learn via WhatsApp that they also had a little problem with the pinion of Martin's machine and had to take a rest for one day. Maybe we'll meet them again. For our inquiries regarding the onward journey of Ulan Bator, we first find positive answers. We will see how it develops.
