Stuttgart - Vladivostok 2018

Day 01, Departure, Stuttgart - Lahntal (Austria)

Date Sa 07 Juli 2018 By Helmut Dittrich, Translation by Julie Herberger-Dittrich Category english Tags Bavaria / Austria / Autobahn / Navi / 1. Week

Packing

The preparations have already taken quite some time and in the end it is always tight schedule. Like this time, the day before I had already stowed everything in the pack and this morning came a few 'little things' and then came what had to come: The pack bag is no longer closing.

So I took the one bag out of the packsack and stretched separately on the motorcycle. This goes well but now I have another separate piece of luggage, which I really wanted to avoid.

Everything ready, one last picture of the odometer reading.

On the way to Charlie I noticed that the right suitcase is too heavily loaded (tire iron, tools, etc.) and I already have some trouble in the curves, because the center of gravity is very high.

Finally we go

I arrived at Charlie's place, we drink a quick coffee and make a departure picture. That gives me a flashback right away. Same place, same motive, 11 years back, departure for our tour to St. Petersburg, Russia

It goes on country roads to Bavaria and we stop in Dillingen.

Navi makes problems

On rural roads we continue and very soon we take a break because Charly's navigation system does not charge. We look at the electrical system and find out that the charging current is 12V for him and 5V for my device. The problem is that a new device with an old cable set was installed with him, which apparently caused the voltage converter to fall by the wayside, because it reduces the on-board voltage from 12V to 5V.

We charge with external battery and USB cable and it can go on, which is only a temporary solution to our problem.

At Augsburg, Germany we drive on the highway and make the route to Lahntal to Gasthof Hammerschmidt near Zell am See where we have booked a night on Booking.com The inn is a bit out of the way. We made a light meal in the room with beer and Zweigelt wine.
