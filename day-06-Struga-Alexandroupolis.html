Stuttgart - Vladivostok 2018

Day 06, Struga (Macedonia) - Alexandroupolis (Greece)

Date Do 12 Juli 2018 By Helmut Dittrich, Translation by Julie Herberger-Dittrich Category english Tags Ohrid-Lake / Macedonia / Greece / 01. Week

We take a quick breakfast and drive towards the Greek border.

We have made a long stage for today and are curious if it will work out well. The road is narrow and confusing. Many trucks and buses that are difficult to pass. We drive along wooded mountains and climb to over 1,200m, which makes the temperatures very pleasant.


wild horses on the road


Last gas station in front of the border with cheap gas

The border crossing to Greece is fast and we find well-developed roads in Greece, which allow us to move forward quickly. The closer we get to the sea, the hotter it gets and on the asphalt band it's easy 40 degrees celsius. We often stop for water and a snack. In a small village we eat Souflaki and learn from the owner that she had lived in Stuttgart and that the economic situation is still difficult in Greece.

We notice that we lost an hour because the time zone has changed and we have one hour later. We accelerate again and reach Alexandroupolis around 8:00 pm and after a drive of about 600 km, not far from the Turkish border. We treat ourselves to a seaside hotel (Ramada Placa Thraki) and relax our tired bones.

The hotel service tells us that she comes from Hannover (Germany) and that here the professional prospects are very limited.
