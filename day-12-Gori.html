Stuttgart - Vladivostok 2018

Day 12, Gori (Georgia)

Date Mi 18 Juli 2018 By Helmut Dittrich, Translation by Julie Herberger-Dittrich Category english Tags Gori / Stalin / Georgia / Uplistsikhe / 2. Week / Silk Road

Also because of the time change we decide to take a rest day.


Hotel in Gori

We visit, not far from Gori the archaeological site Uplistsikhe a former trading center on the Silk Road, built in the 6th century BC. Was founded in the 3rd millennium BC.

Along the way, we see how gas pipelines continue to work. There is also grown a lot of wine production. We are lucky and reach the place before the buses approach endless crowds of tourists from Tbilisi.

We climb around on the sandstone and unfortunately do not get any further information, because we save on explanatory boards and refer to audio guides. We take photos and drink coffee and water as the rush and temperatures get too high. In the meantime the buses are damming and the crowds are getting more and more.

We drive back, eat a bite on l'Avenue, the pub just around the corner and work on our records.

At 5:00 pm we go to the Stalin Museum nearby.

Amazing what personality cult you make of it, considering what he has to answer for. Even the wagon with which he drove through Russia is ready for inspection.

It is awfully humid and it seems to brew something. We open our office in l'Avenue, where the service Maria already knows us and with whom one can communicate well in English. The heavy rain stays out and we work on our records, eat well and treat ourselves to a bottle of Georgian wine. The food and drink, which is in excellent quality, costs about 1/3 of it as in Germany. Fuel price about 0.85 Eur.
