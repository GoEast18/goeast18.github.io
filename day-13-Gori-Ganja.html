Go East

Stuttgart - Vladivostok 2018

Day 13, Gori (Georgia) - Ganja (Azerbaijan)

Date Do 19 Juli 2018 By Helmut Dittrich, Translation by Julie Herberger-Dittrich Category english Tags Georgia / Azerbaijan / Ganja / 02. Week / kickstand repair


Ganja

Contrary to expectations, it does not rain and after breakfast we settle the bill (50, - for 2 persons and 2 nights with breakfast) and go.


Not everything is sad in the backyard!


Not everything is sad in the backyard!

At the end of town we fill up and the operator tells us that he lived for 6 years in Schleswig Holstein (Germany).

Charlie has difficulty switching, as the pin on the kickstand has changed position during the welding action. A workshop nearby is helping and half of the pin is flexing. The workshop would be a treat for the experts in Germany of the company cooperative, occupational safety and certification body. (As I write this I look over to the older gentleman in the mirror and see a soft knowing smile).


kickstand repair


kickstand repair

We set off at moderate temperatures so at 25 degrees, which increase rapidly to over 30 degrees. The highway to Tbilisi is great. Tbilisi is hot. We are now on the road at almost 40 degrees Celsius (104 F) and in the traffic jam we are properly cooked in the city. The traffic is not quite as aggressive as in Istanbul.

Strikingly many right-hand drive vehicles are to be observed. We are glad to be out of the city relatively fast. We gain altitude, the landscape is covered with a fine green.


Over Tbilisi

Just before the border with Azerbaijan we stop at a truck stop, eat scrambled eggs, drink water and tea. Great atmosphere there, as in the Western and again very hot.


Truck stop

We drive on and come to the border control after a few kilometers. The check-in takes a while, but everything runs correctly.

In Azerbaijan the time wheel seems to turn faster than Georgia. Many new buildings, Mercedes and Lada dominate the streetscape. Roads are in good shape. We buy a chicken in a small town from the grill pit, which makes us so tired in the heat, that we stop at a gas station for 1.5 hours.

At 17:30 we drive on. The heat let up and we reach Ganja, where we head for the Ramada and enjoy the pool after this very hot stage today.
