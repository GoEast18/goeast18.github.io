Stuttgart - Vladivostok 2018

Day 72, Ulan Bator (Mongolia)

Date So 16 September 2018 By Helmut Dittrich, Translation by Julie Herberger-Dittrich Category english Tags 11. Week / Mongolia / Ulan-Bator

After sleeping in we eat a big breakfast and have an interesting conversation with a couple from Holland, who are already 15 years on the road with a Toyota Landcruiser and obviously live from writing travelogues for several magazines. There is also an older man from Geneva who is on a journey that will last 5 years with his well kept off-road vehicle.

At 11:00 am TQ comes and we unload the van give him the rest of the money and say goodbye. The carpenter comes to measure the boxes for the motorcycles. The containers will be ordered accordingly by us regarding luggage capacity along with the disassembled front wheel. The motorcycles are placed on pallets and secured with strong moving bands. The pallet is then closed with wooden boards and prepared for transportation. Zirma the manager, who also speaks German and English, is very helpful. She has good connections to the Pan-Europa transportation group which handles our motorcycle transportation.

Our friends Martin and Andre come in and there is a great reunion. We have a lot to talk about and celebrate the occation with a bit of Genghis vodka and Tiger beer.
