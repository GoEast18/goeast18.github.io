Go East

Stuttgart - Vladivostok 2018

Day 46, Sary-Tash (Kyrgyzstan) - Osh (Kyrgyzstan)

Date Di 21 August 2018 By Helmut Dittrich, Translation by Julie Herberger-Dittrich Category english Tags 7. Week / Sarytash / Kyrgyzstan / Osh / silk road

The SIM cards we bought do not work, because obviously they still have to be charged, but we do not understand how it should work.

Screws, oil and tire pressure are ok. The luggage support got a hit and is a bit crooked. Zipper from the tank bag is bad and does not close anymore. We take our time packing and let the others go for the first time. Only Frank and Sylvia stay to recover even further and make the fat bikes clear.


Ainur, the owner of the hostel Murnas and her sister

Around noon we finally start on our journey and we decide to go to Osh first. After refueling we cross two passes and overtake Luke, who has already made 30km on his way to Osh.

Many yurts with herds and shepherds are on the way to see and the landscape changes again from rugged rock formations in the mountains to agricultural land in the mountain valleys.

There is a lot of construction and one gets the impression that here in Kyrgyzstan the projects are approached with more professionalism. Obviously, there is more money available, which is also evident in the quality of the streets and the car brands. Shared taxis are seen often and relatively new Mercedes Sprinters are being driven. There are also many well-preserved cars of European brands on the road. It is striking that people now have Asian facial features, which we noticed for the first time sporadically in Murghab and on Lake Karakul.

A rain wall threatens to overtake us, but it evaporates again until we reach Osh Osh is at 1,000 and it is a bit warmer again and makes a very lively impression on us. We run to the Shanghai City Hotel to enjoy some rest after the very exhausting last few days and many discussions. The manager is accommodating, gives and a generous discount and helps us to activate and replenish the sim cards.

In Dolce Vita, an Italian restaurant well known to travelers, we enjoy what the 'European cuisine and wine cellar' has to offer and return to the hotel very satisfied.
