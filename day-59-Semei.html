Go East

Stuttgart - Vladivostok 2018

Day 59, Semei (Kazakhstan)

Date Mo 03 September 2018 By Helmut Dittrich, Translation by Julie Herberger-Dittrich Category english Tags 09. Week / Kazakhstan / Semei

We enjoy breakfast together with Martin and Andre, then they leave in the 9 degrees and rain. We have to stay in Semi until 9/5/2018, because our visa for Russia is only valid from then on.

Shortly thereafter, we also go and look for the motorcycle workshop, which was specified in the I-Overlander. Unfortunately the address proves to be incorrect and we land in an open field in the pouring rain.

We locate a tire workshop and Charlie's front tire is re-mounted because it was not completely on and slipped out the last time the inner tube was changed because there was too little air compression available.

In search of another workshop, we drive through Semi in the rain and cold. It does not look so appealing with the rundown houses and run-down streets here. Of course, the weather also does its part in making everything here look so depressing today. The heavy rain also has completely overwhelmed the sewer system making the large streets flooded. When riding a motorcycle in the city, one always fears driving in an open man hole, which would certainly not be unusual here.

We encounter another tire workshop that can replace Charlie's broken spoke. Max the owner, goes out of his way to help us and does a great job. When asked what it costs, he just means that he also is a biker and therefore it costs nothing!

We warm up in the hotel restaurant, have a late lunch and spend the rest of the day reading, writing and drinking tea. Through Whats-App we find out that Martin and Andre spent about 3 hours on the border of Russia.
