Go East

Stuttgart - Vladivostok 2018

About us

Date Sa 07 Juli 2018 By Helmut Dittrich / Karl-Heinz Dittmann, Translation by Julie Herberger-Dittrich Category about us Tags Charlie / Helme / HPN / BMW / Silk Road

We are now two gentlemen, ripe in age,

Karl-Heinz Dittmann (Charlie, 63) and Helmut Dittrich (Helme, 61),

who have already made several trips together with our motorcycles throughout the years.


Karl-Heinz Dittmann (left), Helmut Dittrich (right)

After some motorcycle abstention in our 40s, we decided to start biking again. It all restarted about 11 years ago, when we decided to get going again. Together we drove to St. Petersburg (Russia) over the Baltic states, and thus our jouney began. Our next destination was to Turkey. A short time later we traveled around the Ukraine and went Russia (Sochi-Trabzon). We even enjoyed Syria (Palmyra, Damascus, etc.) by motorcycle and luckily got to experience it before the horrible war began.

Those good experiences gave us an appetite for the East.

We tackled Morocco 5 years ago with the motorcycle and finally it worked out this year to begin another great adventure together. We visioned riding the motorcycles through the eastern part of the world all the way to the Japanese Sea, to Vladivostok in Russia.

We will do it,

because we are curious and just go for it ... (you will not get any younger either :-)

The bikes

are rebuilt HPN-BMWs, both built in 1981, which meanwhile have a couple of years and miles under their hoods.

The planned route

Our plan is to travel to as many countries as we can and to follow the Silk Road as much as possible. We did not want to travel to China due to the difficult circumstances and instead decided to cross the Mongolian steppes and finally finish the journey in Vladivostok as our easternmost point.

From there, the motorcycles are then taken by ship to the USA, where we will continue our tour in the coming years (USA - Central and South America).

The itinerary takes us from Germany to Austria and then on to Italy, Slovenia, Croatia, Monte Negro, Albania, (North) Macedonia, Greece, Turkey, Georgia, Azerbaijan, Iran, Turkmenistan, Uzbekistan, Tajikistan, Kyrgyzstan, Kazakhstan, Russia and Mongolia again to Russia, from where we will travel to our last destination in Vladivostok.
