Stuttgart - Vladivostok 2018

Day 28, Tabas (Iran) - Mashhad (Iran)

Date Fr 03 August 2018 By Helmut Dittrich, Translation by Julie Herberger-Dittrich Category english Tags 4. Week / Iran / Tabas / Desert / Desert Kawir / Mashhad / silk road

Today is the last day of the fourth week of our journey. This means that one-third of our planned travel time has already passed and we have completed about 40% of our total journey distance.

We start at 9:30 and the temperatures are moderate with 34 degrees. It continues through the desert and we mainly encounter trucks. We cross mountain foothills and the altitude varies between 1,000m and 2,000m.


Also in the desert there is speed control with video surveillance!

Gonabad we stop at a restaurant. Chicken or sheep is the question again. This time we choose sheep and are very satisfied. We notice that even this time, no knife is with the cutlery, but only a fork and a spoon. In fact, you do not need a knife, because with the spoon you can cut up everything into small portions.


The spoon replaces the knife

Our "soup coma" has overcome us again and we take a nap in the nearby park. Moving on, we renounce the world cultural heritage in Gonabad with its qanatas (system for water production) since we still have a 300 km drive ahead of us and the afternoon heat is not too inviting for a walk.

We wind down with the remaining 300km and take another break to pick up fruit and water.

We head for the Hotel Homa in Mashhad, which is also located at the silk road and are well accommodated, but suddenly realize that we ran out of Iranian money!
