Stuttgart - Vladivostok 2018

Day 34, Samarkand (Uzbekistan)

Date Do 09 August 2018 By Helmut Dittrich, Translation by Julie Herberger-Dittrich Category english Tags 5. Week / Uzbekistan / silk road / Samarkand / tank repair

Write and relax by the pool. The cyclists, a Dutchman and a woman from Hamburg who are traveling with the group, tell us about their tour with tdaglobalcycling.com nd that their tour goes from Beijing to Istanbul. You can go for different stages and that it is already for many people at the limit.


Global cycling

They think that along the Pamir Highway, which is what we want to drive on too, that there are probably no supply stations and that the security situation should be ok.

Charlie is still working on his tank and seals it off because he has a small hairline crack and loses fuel when the tank is full.

At about 5:30 pm we take a taxi and drive to Registan, a square surrounded by 3 15th and 17th century madrasas decorated with majolica ceramics. Here we also climb one of the minarets (the crook on the right) and get a great panoramic view.

The evening light is excellent and it is also a lot of fun to watch all the activity with the many people.

We take a taxi back to yesterday's restaurant and I try the menu of national delicacies. Manti, tongue salad with feta cheese, steamed lamb with potato and pea, carrot vegetables, in addition to Uzbek beer on tap and Uzbek Merlot. Finally, an exquisite apple strudel with a scoop of vanilla ice cream. Delicious.


Restaurant


Restaurant

On the way back to the hotel, the taxi driver tries to rip us off and demands smoothly double the usual fare. We do not begrudge him the experience of success and so he pulls away feeling contrite.
